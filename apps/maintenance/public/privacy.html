Data We Collect and How We Use It

Profile Data: To facilitate connecting you with other users, we display the profile information you provide us (such as your display name, profile pictures, bio, and age) to them, and factor some of this information into our matchmaking algorithm. You may voluntarily provide sensitive information (such as your sexuality and location) to be displayed on your profile. You can control the visibility of some profile data in the Privacy Settings.

Account Data: When you sign up, you provide us with account details (such as your email address) to create your account and authenticate you. We use your email to send you transactional notifications and, with your permission, marketing communications.

Message Data: We process the messages you send in order to facilitate communications with other users.

Purchase Data: If you make a purchase, we collect your transaction details (such as subscription type and billing history) to fulfill your order and comply with accounting requirements. We do not store your full payment card details directly; they are processed securely by our payment partners.

Connected Platform Data: If you connect an external account, we collect information from it (such as your Discord ID or VRChat username). We use this data to enable social sign-in, share your connected profile with matches to facilitate communications, display connected content on your profile, or support other integrations.

Verification Data: If you choose to verify your identity or age, or where required by law, we may process your government-issued ID and/or biometric data (such as photo, video, and face geometry) to confirm your identity.

Usage Data: We collect data about how you use our service (such as when you access it, how you navigate and interact with it). We use this data to provide and improve the service.

Technical Data: We collect data from your device (such as IP address, device/browser identifiers, error/crash data, hardware and software information). We use this data to secure your account, customize your experience, and improve our service.

Additionally, we may use the above data to ensure the safety and security of our platform, prevent fraud, provide customer support, and comply with legal requirements. We may use aggregate or anonymized data to promote our service.

Contractual Necessity: To provide our service (such as matchmaking, messaging, and facilitating social interactions).

Legitimate Interest: To ensure the safety and security of our platform, prevent fraud, and analyze usage to improve our service.

Legal Obligation: To comply with valid legal requests and reporting requirements.

Consent: For sensitive personal information you voluntarily provide (such as sexual orientation, precise geolocation, and biometric data) and for marketing communications. You may withdraw this consent at any time in the Settings.

Automated Profiling

We use an automated matchmaking algorithm to suggest compatible users to you, and to suggest your profile to other users. Our algorithm considers factors including matching profile data (such as interest tags, personality similarity, and geographic proximity), recent activity, and whether a user has already expressed interest in your profile. These factors are prioritized according to a combination of our preset values and, in some cases, user-defined weights. Results are filtered based on your preferences and theirs (such as gender, age, and other filters you choose). We do not use profiling to make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on you.

How Long We Retain Your Data

We retain transaction records, customer support records, and supporting data for 7 years to comply with accounting requirements.

If you verify your identity or age, your ID and biometric data is deleted immediately after the verification is complete.

If your account is suspended for violating our terms, we retain limited data necessary to prevent you from opening a new account (such as your email, usernames, IP address, and device/browser identifiers) and moderation records (such as reports, flagged data, and enforcement actions) for as long as necessary to ensure the safety of our platform.

Otherwise, we retain your data until your account is deleted. If your account remains inactive for two years, we will automatically delete it. If your account is suspended for violating our terms, we will automatically delete it (except the limited safety data described above) after 30 days.

When your account is deleted or you remove specific data from your profile, it is immediately deleted from our production systems. It may persist in disaster recovery snapshots for up to 7 days before being permanently erased.
